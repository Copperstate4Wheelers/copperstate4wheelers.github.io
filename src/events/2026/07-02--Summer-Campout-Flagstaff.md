## Cancelled

A Pocket Fire in the Coconino National Forest has caused significant portions
of the forest to be closed.

Due to this, the club has cancelled this camping trip for now. Stay safe and
have a good 4th of July!

[Forest Service: Coconino Alerts](https://www.fs.usda.gov/r03/coconino/alerts)

## Original Post:

The club will camp near the Edge Of The World in the Coconino National Forest
near Flagstaff. We've camped around here on the 4th of July the last few years
and it has always been a lot of fun. We will probably hit the nearby trails
on multiple days during the weekend, but the official Club Run will be on
Saturday if you prefer only to come up for the day.

More details to come!

### July 4th Activity

On Saturday, July 4th, we'll do a club run to explore the forest around
the campsite and the Edge Of The World. This will not be a traditional run,
but instead a orienteering challenge (map and compass) to find hidden caches
spread out within the forest!

**Date:** July 4th, 2026
**Start time:** 9:00am

You do not need to camp with us to participate, but please let Bradley or
someone on the board know if you plan to come so we can watch for you!
