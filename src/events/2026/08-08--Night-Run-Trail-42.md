Trail 42 is always interesting.
The challenge on this trail comes almost entirely from the many off-camber and
high-articulation spots.

We've never done it at night, but it should be fun!

### Requirements

* [Tonto National Forest OHV Permit](https://www.recreation.gov/activitypass/AP22844)
* [Normal required gear](/about/required-gear)
* Rear locker advised
* Recovery equipment
* flashlights and other adequate lighting for a nighttime run
