This run is almost an annual tradition for us at this point.
The Hieroglyphic Loop is a fun trail with a lot of up and down. There's a great
view of the city once we reach the top.

Expect to be out late on this run. Last year we didn't get off the trail until
a bit after midnight.

### Requirements

* [State Trust land permit](https://azland.my.salesforce-sites.com/recreationalpermit/)
* [Normal required gear](/about/required-gear)
* Rear locker advised
* Recovery equipment
* flashlights and other adequate lighting for a nighttime run
