The club will find a campsite near Wickenburg, run the nearby Box Canyon trail,
and explore other trails in the area.

Box Canyon will start at 9:00am at the Box Canyon Staging Area on Saturday 21st.

Contact Jim for campsite coordinates if you're planning to camp on Friday or
Saturday night.

### Requirements

- State Trust Land Permit
- [Normal required gear](/about/required-gear)
